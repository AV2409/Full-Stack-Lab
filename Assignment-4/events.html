Competitions & Events

Choose your challenge from our wide array of technical and non-technical events.

  • CodeStorm - Competitive Programming

    A 3-hour intense coding challenge. Solve algorithmic problems and race to the top of the leaderboard. Open to individuals.

  • RoboWars - Robotics Combat

    Design, build, and battle your robot in a knockout-style tournament. May the best machine win! Teams of 2-4.

  • InnovateX - Project Showcase

    Present your innovative hardware or software project to a panel of esteemed judges from the industry. Teams of 1-3.

  • DesignaThon - UI/UX Challenge

    A 24-hour design hackathon where participants create compelling user interfaces for a given problem statement. Open to individuals and teams of 2.

  • GameJam - Gaming Tournament

    Compete in popular esports titles like Valorant and FIFA. Form a team and fight for glory and prizes.
